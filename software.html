Research Software

Software released in relation to my research.

ASP-QSIM

ASP-QSIM is an ASP implementation of the QSIM algorithm extended with qualitative rules.
Software is maintained, last updated in 2021. Released as part of the work of: Wiley, T. (2017). A Planning and Learning Hierarchy for the Online Acquisition of Robot Behaviours, School of Computer Science and Engineering, The University of New South Wales, Sydney, Australia.
Publicly available on GitHub at: https://github.com/timothy-wiley/aspqsim
