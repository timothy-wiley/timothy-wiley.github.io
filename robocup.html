RoboCup is the premiere annual international competition for autonomous robotics, featuring teams competing from over 40 countries across a wide variety of challenges. I have been involved in RoboCup for over a decade. My current roles include participating at RMIT and roles within the RoboCup organisation.

RoboCup Soccer SPL

I served as member of the Technical Committee (TC) for the RoboCup Soccer Standard Platform League from 2017 - 2022. The TC is responsible for managing the rules and qualification process for the league.

RedBackBots

I am currently the academic supervisor and team lead for RedBackBots, the RMIT RoboCup Soccer SPL Team. RedBackBots was formed in 2020, and has competed at RoboCup since 2022.
