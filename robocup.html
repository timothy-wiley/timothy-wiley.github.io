RoboCup is the premiere annual international competition for autonomous robotics, featuring teams competing from over 45 countries across a wide variety of challenges. I have been involved in RoboCup for over 15 years. My current roles include participating at RMIT and roles within the RoboCup organisation.

RoboCup Soccer SPL

I am currently a member of the Executive Committee for the RoboCup Soccer Standard Platform League. My current term is from 2024-2028.
I was as member of the Technical Committee (TC) for the RoboCup Soccer Standard Platform League from 2017 - 2022.

RedBackBots

I am currently the academic supervisor and team lead for RedBackBots, the RMIT RoboCup Soccer SPL Team. More information about our team, including how to join, is available on our homepage. RedBackBots was formed in 2020, and has competed at RoboCup since 2022. Our results are:

Year Location Result
2024 Eindhoven, The Netherlands 3rd Place, Challenge Shield (Division 2)
2023 Bordeaux, France 5th Place, Challenge Shield (Division 2)
2022 Bangkok, Thailand (Competed Remotely) 8th Technical Challenges. Best result: 3rd Visual Referee Challenge
